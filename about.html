About

Overview

A full-service platform.

Silverwood is a real estate investment, development, and advisory firm focused on ground-up development across multiple asset classes.

The firm works end-to-end — from site selection and acquisitions through underwriting, entitlements, financing, design, permitting, construction, and asset management. Silverwood operates primarily in Western U.S. markets.
