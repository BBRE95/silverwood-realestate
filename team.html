Brandon Becker
Founder & President

Brandon is the Founder of Silverwood Real Estate, a real estate investment, development, and advisory firm focused on delivering thoughtfully designed and financially sound projects.

With over eight years of experience in acquisitions, entitlements, and development across high-barrier Western markets, Brandon brings a sharp analytical mind and a hands-on leadership style to every project.

Prior to launching Silverwood, Brandon served as Vice President of Acquisitions & Development at Allied Urban, where he led the sourcing and underwriting of more than 400 investment opportunities and managed over $400 million in active development across multifamily, mixed-use, office conversions, and self-storage. His responsibilities spanned the full project lifecycle — from financial modeling and capital raising through entitlements, design and permitting, and asset management.

Earlier in his career, Brandon held key roles at LaTerra Development, where he underwrote more than 500 development sites and worked on the acquisition and financing of a development portfolio exceeding $1 billion. His experience navigating entitlements, financial structuring, and stakeholder engagement has made him a trusted partner to investors, lenders, and municipalities.
